About GUTTER

The representative bowling club of Pukyong National University, "Gutter," is a central club that has existed since 1988. If you join the gutter, you can participate in a competition hosted by Busan University, Pukyong University, Dong-A University, Dong-seo University, Dong-eui University, and Dong-myeong University, which are universities in Busan. Join the gutter to make new friends in school and interact with friends from other schools!
